About

Hi I'm Justin Kraus - data analyst and visualization specialist based out of Brooklyn New York. I like to display abstractions of datasets by finding patterns of information, ideally through dynamic web experiences. I strive for engaging presentations that allow for quick understanding as well as providing the ability to provide the user tools for deeper exploration if wanted. I'm passionate about exploring cultural datasets to better understand what people like, observing trends and trying to define what makes things objectively good. I strive for efficiency and optimization in everything I do. I've worked in Financial Services for 10+ years in roles related to financial analysis and data management.
